## How to request account deletion

You can delete your account in two ways:

### Option 1 — In the app

1. Open 916TV.
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** (or **Edit Profile**).
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm. Your deletion request is queued and will be fully processed within 30 days. After confirmation you are immediately signed out and your account is no longer accessible.

### Option 2 — By email

If you cannot access the app, email **abdujabbordeveloper@gmail.com** from the email address registered on your account with the subject line **"Delete my 916TV account"**. We will process the request within 30 days and confirm by email when complete.

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your profile information (full name, email, date of birth, profile photo).
- Your authentication data (password hash, OAuth identifiers from Google/Apple Sign-In).
- Your activity data (likes, saved items, watch history, archived items).
- Your user-generated content (comments, posts, uploaded media).
- Your push notification tokens (FCM tokens).

## What data is retained

For legal, fraud-prevention, and operational reasons, we may retain the following for a limited period:

- **Transactional and security logs** — up to 90 days, for fraud detection and abuse investigations.
- **Aggregated, anonymized analytics** — retained indefinitely; cannot be linked back to you.
- **Records required by law** — retained for the period required by applicable Uzbek law (typically up to 3 years for tax and regulatory records, where applicable).

## Timeline

- **In-app deletion request:** account is deactivated immediately; full data removal completes within 30 days.
- **Email deletion request:** processed within 30 days.
- **Backup purge:** retained backups containing your data are overwritten within 90 days of deletion.
